<h2 id="navigation">Navigation</h2>
<p>
In addition to the usual Android gestures and navigation, SolitaireCG
includes the following features:<br>
<br>
<b>Moving</b><br>
Single Card: Drag the card.<br>
Whole Stack: Drag the stack starting above the bottom card.<br>
Partial Stack: Tap the stack, scroll to adjust, then drag the top card.<br>
<br>
<b>Auto Move</b><br>
Fling a card to start a chain of moving cards to their home.<br>
<br>
<b>Dealing</b><br>
Tap the deck or deal menu, or click the trackball or search key.<br>
<br>
<b>Undo</b><br>
Push the back button.<br>
<br>
<b>Menu</b><br>
Change the game type, restart, deal, change the options, view stats or help.<br>
Long press bottom center of screen for alternate menu.
</p>
